## Selected solutions following RICE analysis

* **Filtering 2.0**
* **Simple Linear Dependencies** (Blocks / Is Blocked)
* **Simple Label System**

---

### Rationale

The main reason scaling teams churn is FlowCraft's inability to support cross-team work â€“ specifically the lack of initiative visibility (**Opportunity #1**) and dependency management (**Opportunity #2**).

To genuinely impact retention in this segment, we must deliver solutions that directly address these two problems. Looking at our priority list, the key combination appears to be:

1.  **3C: Filtering 2.0 (Rank 1)**
    * Enables access to cross-team data.
2.  **2A: Simple Linear Dependencies (Blocks / Is Blocked) (Rank 2)**
    * Enables dependency management.
3.  **1A: Simple Label System (Aggregated View) (Rank 4)**
    * Enables data aggregation for a "bird's-eye view" when combined with filtering.

#### The Role of Recurring Tasks (4B)

> This solution has a high RICE score because it solves a common, annoying pain point. Its implementation will likely improve general satisfaction and may reduce churn in other segments.

However, it does not directly address the main reason why 30-50 person teams leave us (cross-team coordination issues). Therefore, implementing **4B** alone, despite its high RICE score, will likely not be enough to reach the 90% retention goal in this specific segment.
